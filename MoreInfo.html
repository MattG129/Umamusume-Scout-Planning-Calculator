Genshin Wish Planning Calculator

About


This calculator estimates how many wishes users could save up for certain characters and weapons and uses wish simulations to approximate the chances of acquiring them. Due to the use of simulations there can be some slight deviations in the results, but they shouldn't be off by more than half a percent. Primogems from Dailies, Character Trials, Live Streams, and Maintenance are factored into calculations, despite not having any configuration options.
